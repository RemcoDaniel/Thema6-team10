motorTask - checks and maintains the correct speed and direction of the motor
buttonTask - checks if the button is pushed
heaterTask - checks and maintains the correct temprature.
pumpTask - pumps water in or out
ledTask - controls whether the led is on or not
webSocketTask - sends and recieves requests and answeres to/from the webbrowser

measurementControlTask - periodicly checks each unit - weight, temprature, filled water
inputControlTask - checks input and sends out signals to other objects
wasmachineControlTask - takes signals and sends other signals out

loggingTask - logs data from all areas

// Regulating speed, temprature and water level
wasmachineControlTask -> motorTask, heaterTask, pumpTask
regulateWasmachineprogramTask

// Requesting speed, temprature and water level and misc
loggingTask -> measurementControlTask -> motorTask, pumpTask, heaterTask
loggingTask

// Sending and recieving data over the internet
wasmachineControlTask -> websocketTask
webCommunicationTask

// Sending and recieving data over the uart

uartCommunicationTask
